Instalation Guide

It is recommended to install via the Google Play app directly
Option 1 Google Play app
Option 2 Google Play website
Option 3 Phone companion app

Via Google Play app

1. Open the Google Play page of the watch face you want to install on your phone. 2. Click the arrow next to the install button.
3. Make sure that your watch is selected. 4. Click the install button on the bottom of the screen.
5. The watch face will be installed on your watch shortly.

Via Google Play website

1. Copy the download link of the watch face and open it in a web browser 2. Make sure that you are logged in to your Google account. 3. Click on “install on more devices”
4. Make sure that your watch is selected. 5. Click the install button.
6. The watch face will be installed on your watch shortly.

Via phone companion app

1. Open the Google Play page of the watch face you want to install on your phone. 2. Click on the install button. 3. Wait for the installation to finish on your phone and open the app. 4. Click on the “install on wear device” button in the app.
5. The Play Store page of the watch face will be opened on your watch, install the watch face from there. 6. The watch face will be installed on your watch shortly.
